TOP QUERIES

The graduate placement program typically runs from November to March. The summer internship placements are in November-December, Lateral/Experienced hiring in February and Full-time placement in January and March. For specific dates in the current year please write to your relationship manager. If you are a first time recruiter, please register to our website to know more. You will be assigned a relationship manager who will be your one-point contact .

The placement guide and student profile book for the year is typically available for dispatch/download in October. For a printed copy of the placement brochure and profile book please e-mail your relationship manager or write to the Placement Website email.

The information that students typically look for in corporate presentations is: Profile of the company Profile of the career role (projects in case of summer internships) and responsibilities Locations where the student is likely to be posted Compensation / Stipend


INFORMATION QUERIES

Students can be recruited for: A two-month full-time summer internship Experienced/Lateral positions Entry-level full-time roles.

The typical steps in the placement process would be Communication of interest in the placement program to your relationship manager Finalizing a placement slot Submission of CVs/Applications by students Shortlisting of students Pre-processes (tests/group discussions) Interviews Selection list
